Frequently Asked Questions

How much does moving cost in Edmonton?

Moving costs vary based on distance, volume of items, and additional services. Our local moves typically range from $300-$800 for a 1-2 bedroom apartment. Get a free, no-obligation quote by filling out our form.

What's included in your moving service?

Our standard service includes loading, transportation, and unloading. We also offer packing services, furniture disassembly/reassembly, and specialty item handling for an additional fee.

How far in advance should I book movers?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance, especially during peak moving seasons (summer months and end of month). Last-minute moves may be accommodated based on availability.

Do you provide packing materials?

Yes, we offer high-quality packing materials including boxes, bubble wrap, and packing paper. These can be included in your moving package or purchased separately.
